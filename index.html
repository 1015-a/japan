JAPAN
にほん

Tokyo is an island nation in East Asia, consisting of four main islands (Honshu, Hokkaido, Kyushu, and Shikoku). Its capital is Tokyo. It's known for its blend of traditional culture and cutting-edge technology. 75% of its land is mountainous. With a population of approximately 123 million, it is one of the world's leading economic powers.
